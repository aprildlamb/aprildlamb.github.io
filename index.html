Welcome!

I am currently serving as the Scientific Graphics Quality Coordinator for the Assessments Technical Support Unit (TSU) at the North Carolina Institute for Climate Studies (NCICS). Prior to joining NCICS, I spent eight years working in research and collections at UNC Charlotte, NC State University, and the North Carolina Museum of Natural Sciences. I am a former National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow and hold a Master's degree in Biology, a Graduate Certificate in Geographic Information Systems, and a Bachelor's degree in Zoology from NC State University.
