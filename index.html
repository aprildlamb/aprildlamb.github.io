Welcome!

I am currently a Research Technician in the Department of Bioinformatics and Genomics at UNC Charlotte under Dr. Alex Dornburg . Prior to this position, I received my Masters degree (M.Sc.) in Biology: Ecology and Evolution and a professional certificate in Geospatial Information Systems (GIS) from North Carolina State University. I am also a 2017 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow and hold a Bachelor's degree in Zoology from NC State. Broadly, I am interested in better understanding the bases of ecosystem resiliency (i.e. what factors contribute to an ecosystem's ability to recover from perturbations) and applying this knowledge to inform science-based policy and management decisions. I am especially interested in systems that are experiencing high levels of anthropogenically-driven change.
