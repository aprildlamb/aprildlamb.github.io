Welcome!

This page houses various manuscript figures that I have designed over the past 8 or so years. Most, if not all, of these figures were created in either the statistical programming software R or GIS and then imported into Adobe Illustrator. Each figure includes a caption stating who or what project the figure was designed for and, if applicable, a link to the published text.
